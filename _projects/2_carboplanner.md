Co-founded CarboPlanner, a sports nutrition platform used by elite football clubs and competitive athletes. Raised 1,090,000 DKK in funding. Nominated for SDU Best Startup.

## Stack

- **Web:** React with TypeScript
- **Mobile:** Flutter (iOS and Android)
- **Backend:** NestJS with MongoDB
- **Infrastructure:** Docker, CI/CD pipelines

## What It Does

CarboPlanner generates personalized meal plans based on an athlete's training schedule, body composition goals, and dietary preferences. The platform calculates macro and micronutrient targets dynamically, adjusting for training load and competition schedules.

## Scale

- 100+ athletes across multiple elite clubs
- iOS and Android apps in production
- Real-time sync between web dashboard (for coaches) and mobile app (for athletes)
